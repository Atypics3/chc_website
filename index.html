About Us

As an computer hardware club, we will be doing anything computer hardware related. Specifically Of course, we will be doing and have done things from other aspects of computers such as programming games, learning how to hack, and analyzing networks.
Here are some of the activities:

  • Computer and Internet Safety
  • Software and Operation System Installations
